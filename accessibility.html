Accessibility Statement

Everyone is welcome at Bukas — online and in person.

Bukas Cafe is committed to making our website and our cafe accessible to as many people as possible, including people with disabilities.

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Our goal

We aim to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA as a practical standard. Accessibility is an ongoing effort, and we work to improve the experience for all visitors over time.

What we do on this website

  • Use clear, readable text with strong color contrast.
  • Provide descriptive alternative text for meaningful images.
  • Support keyboard navigation, so the site can be used without a mouse.
  • Use proper headings and structure so screen readers can make sense of each page.
  • Label forms and buttons clearly.
  • Keep the layout responsive so it works across phones, tablets, and computers.

At our cafe

We want every guest to feel at home at 56-14 Van Horn St. If there's anything we can do to make your visit more comfortable, please let us know — we're happy to help.

Known limitations

Some third-party content, such as embedded maps, may not be fully accessible because it is provided by outside services. We do our best to offer alternatives, like a text address and a directions link.

We'd love your feedback

If you run into any difficulty using our website, or you have suggestions on how we can do better, please reach out. Your feedback genuinely helps us improve, and we'll do our best to provide the information or assistance you need.
