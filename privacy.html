Privacy Policy

How we handle your information — in plain English.

Bukas Cafe ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have.

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Who we are

Bukas Cafe is a coffee shop located at 56-14 Van Horn St, Elmhurst, NY 11373. You can reach us anytime using the contact details at the bottom of this page.

What we collect

Information you give us

  • Email address — when you sign up for our mailing list ("The Bukas List").
  • Anything you send us — for example, if you email or message us directly.

Information collected automatically

  • Basic, privacy-friendly analytics — we use Vercel Web Analytics to understand which pages are popular and how the site performs. It does not use cookies and does not track you across other websites.
  • Standard server logs — like most websites, our host may record technical details such as your browser type and approximate location for security and reliability.

How we use your information

  • To send you news, new drinks and menu drops, early-hours updates, and the occasional regular-only perk.
  • To respond to your questions and messages.
  • To keep the site secure and improve how it works.

We will only email you about Bukas Cafe. We don't send your messages anywhere they don't need to go.

Who we share it with

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We share it only with trusted service providers who help us run the cafe and this website, such as:

  • Our email marketing provider, which stores your email address and delivers our newsletter on our behalf.
  • Our website host and analytics provider, which keep the site online and running well.

These providers are only permitted to use your information to provide their service to us. We may also disclose information if required by law.

Your choices

  • Unsubscribe anytime — every newsletter includes an unsubscribe link. One click and you're off the list.
  • Access or deletion — you can ask us what information we hold about you, or ask us to delete it, by contacting us below.

How long we keep it

We keep your email address until you unsubscribe or ask us to remove it. After that, we delete it from our active mailing list.

Children

Our website is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll change the "Last updated" date above. Significant changes will be noted clearly.
